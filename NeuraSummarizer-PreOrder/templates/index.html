Frequently Asked Doubts

Q: How do I upload my documents?

A: Simply drag and drop your PDF, DOCX, or TXT files into the uploader. You can also browse your computer to select the file directly.

Q: What kind of documents can I upload?

A: Currently, we support PDFs, DOCX (Microsoft Word), and TXT files. You can upload any document in these formats, regardless of their content.

Q: Can I export the summaries to other formats?

A: Yes! Once your document is summarized, you can export the summary to a variety of formats including PDF, DOCX, TXT, and even Excel or CSV for data extraction purposes.

Q: How accurate is the AI summarization?

A: Our AI model is trained on a vast range of documents, from research papers to legal contracts. While it is highly accurate, you can always review and fine-tune the summaries for any specifics you're concerned about.

Q: Is my data safe with NeuraSummarizer?

A: Absolutely! We prioritize your privacy and security. All data is encrypted end-to-end, and we ensure that free-tier users' data is automatically deleted after processing. For paid users, we also offer enterprise-grade security options with on-premise solutions.

Q: How long does the summarization take?

A: The time it takes depends on the length and complexity of your document. For most standard documents, it should only take a few seconds to a minute. Longer or more complex documents may take a bit longer to analyze.
